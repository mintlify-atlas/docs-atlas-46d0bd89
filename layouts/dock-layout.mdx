---
title: "DockLayout"
description: "A layout that docks child views to the edges or fills the remaining space."
---

`DockLayout` arranges its children by docking them to the top, bottom, left, or right edges of the container. One child can fill the remaining space in the center.

## Basic usage

```xml
xmlns:toolkit="http://schemas.microsoft.com/dotnet/2022/maui/toolkit"

<toolkit:DockLayout>
  <!-- Top bar -->
  <Label toolkit:DockLayout.DockPosition="Top"
         Text="Header"
         BackgroundColor="#04a4ec"
         TextColor="White"
         Padding="8" />

  <!-- Bottom bar -->
  <Label toolkit:DockLayout.DockPosition="Bottom"
         Text="Footer"
         BackgroundColor="#333"
         TextColor="White"
         Padding="8" />

  <!-- Left panel -->
  <BoxView toolkit:DockLayout.DockPosition="Left"
           Color="LightGray"
           WidthRequest="80" />

  <!-- Fills remaining center space (Last child with no dock or Fill) -->
  <ContentView toolkit:DockLayout.DockPosition="None"
               BackgroundColor="White">
    <Label Text="Main content" Margin="16" />
  </ContentView>
</toolkit:DockLayout>
```

## Properties

<ParamField path="DockLayout.DockPosition" type="DockPosition">
  Attached property. Sets where a child is docked. Values: `Top`, `Bottom`, `Left`, `Right`, `None` (fills remaining space).
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="HorizontalSpacing" type="double">
  Space between horizontally adjacent children. Default: `0`.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="VerticalSpacing" type="double">
  Space between vertically adjacent children. Default: `0`.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="ShouldExpandLastChild" type="bool">
  When `true`, the last child fills remaining space. Default: `true`.
</ParamField>

<Note>
  Children are laid out in the order they appear in XAML. Dock the fixed-size edges first (Top, Bottom, Left, Right), then place the fill child last.
</Note>
